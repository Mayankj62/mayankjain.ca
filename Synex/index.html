About

Who we are The next generation of healthcare

Synex Medical is a Toronto-based startup developing the next generation of biomarker monitoring technology. Using magnetic resonance, we can measure blood metabolites like glucose, cholesterol, ketones and lactate, completely non-invasively. This means no needles or finger pricks.


Our vision is to give anyone the ability to know themselves at the molecular level. We want to create a future where getting the data is as simple as putting on a ring.

Measure Monitoring biomarkers in real-time

Glucose

Monitoring blood-glucose levels is an essential practice for people with diabetes to prevent and manage hypo- and hyperglycemia.

Ketones

For people on diets and with diabetes, measuring ketone concentrations is extremely important to prevent ketoacidosis.

Cholesterol

Managing HDL and LDL cholesterol levels is necessary to keep good health and prevent cardiovascular problems like heart disease.

Metabolic rate

Using measurements from glucose and blood gases, we can determine a person's metabolic rate, one of the most reliable measures for general health.

Lactate

Measuring blood-lactate is an important marker in determining fitness performance, and can help to prevent lactic acidosis in critically ill patients.

Alcohol

Having an accurate and real-time measure of blood-alcohol concentration is critical for helping people stay safe at all times.

Technology

One Ring

Our patented technology will come in the form of a wearable ring. This will pair with a companion app on your smartphone or tablet to deliver constant updates on your biomarker measurements along with helpful information.
With a sleek, miniaturized design, this ring will integrate seamlessly into your daily activities, so you can feel comfortable in every moment.

The Technology

We’ve designed a miniaturized magnetic biosensor that can measure the magnetic properties of different biomarkers with high sensitivity and selectivity. This allows for measurement accuracies at the same level as invasive monitors.
By only using magnetic fields and radio pulses, we’re able to perform all these measurements in a completely non-invasive way.
