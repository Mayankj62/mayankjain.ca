Grimm Writer

Writing Fables using Artificial Intelligence


Grimm Writer is a deep neural network that uses LSTM (Long Short Term ) layers to give it the power to generate stories based on the writing style of fables written by the Grimm Brothers.




How it Works

 

Dataset Acquisition

I wanted to create stories based on the Grimm Brothers' writing as they wrote a significant number of Disney's stories. I found a copy of one of their story books online.

Data Processing

The text file full of the story was stripped of non-story material. Then the story was split by words to create a vocabulary for the AI. Any rarely occuring words were removed to speed up training.

Training Data Creation

To train the AI, it was fed a series of words, and trained to detect the next word in that series. The data was one-hot-encoded so that the AI could predict a probability of each word appearing next.

Neural Network Architecture

The neural network uses a bi-directional LSTM. This means that it will use what comes before and after a word to give it more context for a prediction.

Network Training

The network was trained for 200 epochs. Overtime the model gradually improved its ability to create sentences.
